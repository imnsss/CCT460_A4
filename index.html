A1 - Simple Drawing

"Toronto Skyline"

Brief

Replicate a photo by using p5js regular and irregular shapes. This work explores my ability and flexibility in applying p5js functions. I chose a photo I had captured on Toronto Central Island. This photo includes multiple shapes and is a good practice for understanding p5js.
