A3 - Playing with a Tamagotchi!

"Doge!"

Brief

When you come home, you find there is a cute dog in your bedroom! You don't know his name but you can play with him and feed him!

I bought this assets 3 years ago and used the assets because I found it is hard to draw cute Tamagotchi by p5js shapes (my skill is limited). Therefore, I used the cute doge assets and focus more on the function implement. Now the doge has hunger and happiness bar. The doge will feel bad if he starves!! Left click to feed him and right click to give him a bone! Hold Space can make him move!!

Key Features

  • Interactive Design (you can feed and play with the doge)
  • Stats Display (check Doge's status)
  • Dynamic reactions (Doge will be emotional when he feels hungry)
